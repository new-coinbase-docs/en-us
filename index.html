How to Log in to Coinbase Pro


  1. Visit the Coinbase Pro Website
    Go to the official Coinbase Pro website at pro.coinbase.com.
  2. Enter Your Credentials
    Click on the “Sign In” button at the top right corner. Enter your email address and password.
  3. Enable 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)
    For enhanced security, Coinbase Pro will prompt you to verify your identity via Two-Factor Authentication. Use your authentication app or SMS verification.
  4. Access Your Dashboard
    After verification, you’ll have access to your Coinbase Pro dashboard, where you can trade, view charts, and monitor your portfolio.
